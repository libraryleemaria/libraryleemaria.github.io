Maria Lee (she/her) is a recent graduate of the School of Library and Information Science at Simmons University. She also holds a BA in Geography from the University of Minnesota - Twin Cities.

She has worked in a variety of digital library and archive settings including the US National Transportation Library, the Arthur and Elizabeth Schlesinger Library on the History of Women in America, and the Network of the National Library of Medicineâ€™s National Center for Data Services.

Maria was drawn to study archives and records management after working in the Minneapolis Department of Civil Rights and observing the politics of documentation firsthand. Her graduate studies have sharpened her commitment to information justice and helped her discover her professional interests in digital repositories, research data management, and community-engaged scholarship.

Maria grew up in Madison, WI. She now lives in Columbus, OH. You can often find her bopping around town on her bike, at the dog park, or looking at birds by the Olentangy River.
